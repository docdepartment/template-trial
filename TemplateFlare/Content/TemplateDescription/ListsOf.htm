Lists of...

Lists of... are lists that are generated at build time. The content of the list is defined by a paragraph style.

The List of... are available in: Content > Common > Front and Back Matter

The folloinw Lists of... are available:

ListOfFigures

Generates a list of all p.figtitle paragraphs in the document

ListOfTables

Generates a list of all p.ttitle paragraphs in the document

The look of the entries in the lists are controlled by the style MadCap | ListOfProxy.
