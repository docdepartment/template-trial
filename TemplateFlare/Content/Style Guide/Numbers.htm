Numbers

Numbers can be used instead of words in certain cases but not in others.

In body text, use words for numbers one to nine where the number does not precede a unit of measure.

Use numerals for numbers 10 and above.

For round numbers of 1 million or more, use a numeral plus the word.

In measurements, dimensions and cross-references, always use numerals.

Express fractions as a decimal wherever possible.

Use an initial zero for fractions less than one.

Do not use comma notation for thousands and hundreds. The commas can be confused with a series list.

1,000, 100,000, 45,000

1000, 100000, 45000
