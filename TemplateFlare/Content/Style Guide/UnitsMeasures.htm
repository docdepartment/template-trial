Units and measurements

If there is any ambiguity or error in your notation, there could be serious consequences.

There is no space between the number and the unit.

1000V, 100mm, 34m, 23.6A, 56.9KM

1000 V, 100 mm, 34 m, 23.6 A, 56.9 KM

When using the full name of the unit, leave a space between the number and the name.

200 volts, 24 Ohms, 780 amps, 34 watts

200volts, 24Ohms, 780amps, 34watts

Do not use hyphens to indicate a series of numbers.

100-200

100 to 200

Use decimal kilogram units (to one decimal place) for weights over 100 grams. For weights under 100 grams, only use grams.

0.5Kg

500g

28g

Unit abbreviations must comply to the International System of Units.
