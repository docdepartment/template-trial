Facts and figures

Any facts or figures used, or quoted, in technical documentation must be verified and confirmed.

Avoid ambiguity.

The camera has two or three outputs

The camera has two outputs. However, the camera has three outputs when...

Do not use estimates or exaggerations.

The application produces 1000's of results.

The application starts after 10 minutes or so.

The application produces more than 1500 results.

The application produces a large quantity of results.
