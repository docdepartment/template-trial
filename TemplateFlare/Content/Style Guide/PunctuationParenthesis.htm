Parenthesis

Parenthesis is a word or phrase that is inserted into a sentence which would remain grammatically correct without it. Usually parenthesis text is marked using brackets.

Use parentheses to set off incidental or defining information within a sentence or paragraph.

Do not overuse parentheses as this breaks the flow of information. The reader has to stop the reading-flow to absorb the extra information.

Use parentheses when referring to caption items.

Use the camera (1) and the camera housing (2) with the camera mounting plate (3).

Avoid using parentheses for optional plurals.
