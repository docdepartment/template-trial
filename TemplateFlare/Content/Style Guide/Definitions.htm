Definitions

Definitions give a description of a concept or term used in the guide. They can be used in index entries, and help users to identify the concept and recognise other cases, and to differentiate the concept from other similar concepts.

Concepts can be differentiated from other concepts in a category using a description of critical attributes.

A definition should be in the following form:

A [name of concept] is [name of a larger category to which the concept belongs] that [attribute], [attribute]...
