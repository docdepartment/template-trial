Concepts

A concept topic gives a description or explanation of part of the product. It helps to introduce or clarify features which the user may not be familiar with.

Overviews or abstracts are a sub-type of concept topics.

A concept should include phrases such as:

• You can <...> with <the feature>
• Use <the feature> to...
• <The feature> is designed to provide...
• <The feature> includes...
