Trademarks

Trademarks are marks added to product or company names that have been registered with the necessary legal authorities. They are used to protect intellectual property and potential copyright infringements.

Typically this is shown in documentation using a trademark symbol (™) or registered trademark symbol (®) or copyright symbol (©).

• Refer to the client's own Style Guide or branding guidelines on when to use these symbols.
• Do not use these symbols without confirmation.
• The legal front matter in a document can explicitly explain what each trademark is for and when it was registered.
• Avoid using trademarks in content. Use in the front matter where possible.
